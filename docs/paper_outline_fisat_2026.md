# Paper Outline for FISAT 2026

## Title

Explainable Quantum-Inspired Multi-label Classification of Non-Functional Requirements

## Abstract

Software requirements, especially non-functional requirements, are often ambiguous and overlapping across quality attributes. This paper proposes a quantum-inspired semantic representation for multi-label NFR classification. Requirements are represented as semantic states, and NFR labels are modeled as projection dimensions. The model estimates label probabilities through projection amplitudes and provides explanations through feature-level amplitude contributions. Experiments on public NFR datasets compare the proposed model with traditional machine learning, transformer-based, and LLM-based baselines.
